Snake Facts

I've put here a few facts about snakes that I found really cool.
I hope you like them!

Venom Attacks

Ever wondered if snakes can die from venom? Well, they cannot die from their own species' venom, but can if other snake species try to bite them

Food

Believe it or not, snakes can lower their metabolism rate when food is scare. They can live up to 2 years without food. If food is still scare, they can even digest their heart!

Two headed snakes- are they real?

In fact, two-headed snakes are real. Due to a rare disorder called 'polycephaly', each snake head has it's own brain and they often don't agree with each other. This is one of the reasons they very quickly die in the wild.

Do snakes have ears?

Well...yes, as a matter of fact, they do. Although snakes don't have external ears, they do have internal ones.

Where are snakes found?

Snakes are found all over the world, except Antartica.

How large a prey can snakes eat?

Snakes have extremely flexible jaws, which allow them to eat prey bigger than themselves!
